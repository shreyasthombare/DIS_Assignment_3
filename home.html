Montgomery County Recreation Spring Gym Activities

Montgomery County Recreation has 21 recreation centers located throughout Montgomery County that offers a wide range of activities for children, teens, and adults.

Activities Include:

Classes Workshops Clinics Drop-in programs
Supervised play Sports Open Gym Craft

Our centers have diverse amenities that includes weight rooms, social halls, meeting spaces, classrooms, billiards and game rooms. We have a wide array of rooms available for use, from social halls to meeting rooms. You must obtain a Recreation Permit in order to avail these facilities.
